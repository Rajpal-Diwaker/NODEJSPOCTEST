What to expect from unlock.fit

 

1.        Depending on the unlock.fit Product you have purchased, your result could reveal anything from your training intensity response, aerobic trainability and recovery capacity, to a detailed breakdown of your macro-and micro nutrient needs, diet type recommendations, and caffeine and alcohol sensitivities, etc. These will allow you to make genetically informed choices for your lifestyle.

If you have any specific concerns related to health status, genetic testing or lifestyle changes in relation to your own personal health then please consult with a qualified healthcare professional.
